dad-a-base

dad-a-base is a website that scrapes dad-related news and articles from the top dad websites. It helps users find parenting tips, activities to do with their kids, and allows them to connect with others with a built-in forum. Users can log in (via e-mail or Facebook), create discussions, and respond to others' posts.

Summon Simulator

Summon Simulator is a tool for players of Nintendo's hit mobile game Fire Emblem Heroes. It conducts a number of trials to tell the user their chance of randomly summoning their chosen heroes as well as other statistics.

The Neverending Dead

The Neverending Dead is a turn-based combat RPG where you battle against the zombies of past players all the way to the end, where you fight the infamous King of thd Dead.

Blackjack

Play double-deck blackjack with basic strategy tips along the way. You will be told what the optimal move is if the incorrect move is pressed. A suggestion button will also be available if you're unsure of what to do next. The Android version has a tutorial for those who wish to learn "basic strategy," or the optimal way to play each given hand.
